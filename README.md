# Amazon India Sales Analytics
### Python | Pandas | Seaborn | Matplotlib | Power BI

## Overview
End-to-end sales analytics project analyzing 
1,21,180 Amazon India fashion orders across 
9 categories, 36 states, and 3 months 
(Apr–Jun 2022).

## Key Business Insights
1. **Sets dominate revenue** — 50.3% of total 
   revenue (₹3.92 Cr) with highest AOV ₹833
2. **Seasonal risk** — April MoM spike of 2,736%, 
   97% revenue concentrated in 3 months
3. **Geographic gap** — Maharashtra + Karnataka 
   = 47% of orders, North India underpenetrated
4. **Untapped B2B** — only 0.7% of orders are 
   B2B despite higher AOV potential
5. **Plus-size opportunity** — M/L/XL = 49% of 
   revenue, 4XL-6XL = only ₹13.4L total
